About Me

I am a computer science researcher specializing in machine learning and Deep Learning and Bio-informatics. My work focuses on developing robust and interpretable models for real-world applications. Currently, I am an Assistant Professor at the Madanapalle Institute of Technology and Science (MITS).

I am Pursuing my Ph.D. from SRM University, Supervised by Dr. Arulmurugan A. Before that, I completed my M.Tech., at Sathyabama University, Chennai.
